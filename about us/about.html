Find Out !

I met Rex - a very smart dog in our corps. He is an intelligent and courageous boy.

This smart boy accompanied with me in many missions and saved my life so many times. He is not only a friend, he is my family.

I used to think if just only me has this feeling but no, my comrades have the same feeling with their dogs. Isn't it incredible ?

After serving in the army, I returned and studied more about the dogs I had met and researched for new breeds as well. I did share about this with my friend - Chad and he is a vet doctor and he was so happy with the idea and supported it.

That's the reason I published this website to share about my experience as well as puppy care tips for everyone with the consultance from Chad.

Thank you for visiting our website.

We hope you can find valuable information about dogs here and please share with us more about your story with your friend.

If you can't find an answer for your question right here, please feel free to send us a question, we will get back to you very soon.
